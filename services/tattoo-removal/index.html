How It Works

The Science Behind It

Laser energy is swiftly absorbed by the tattoo ink, inducing a mechanical photo-acoustic effect that disintegrates the pigment — causing the tattoo to naturally fade. The body's lymphatic and blood mechanisms then clear the released ink particles from your system. Our Q-Switched YAG laser targets all ink colours while preserving surrounding tissue.
